Annual radiation

The unit for absorbed dose is gray (1 Gy = 1 J/kg). Effective dose takes the absorbed dose (see above) and adjusts it for radiation type and relative organ sensitivity. The annual radiation dose limit for workers is 0.05 sieverts (1 Sv~1 Gy).
To reach the annual radiation dose limit, if the charge of each shot is 1 pC,
$$ \begin{cases} N_{1}=0.05/2.38\times 10^{-8}\approx 2.1\times 10^{6}, & with\ magnet, & without\ beam\ dump\\ N_{2}=0.05/3.17\times 10^{-8}\approx 1.6\times 10^{6}, & without\ magnet, & without\ beam\ dump\\ N_{3}=0.05/8.28\times 10^{-10}\approx 6.0\times 10^{7}, & with\ magnet, & with\ beam\ dump\\ N_{4}=0.05/1.88\times 10^{-9}\approx 2.7\times 10^{7}, & without\ magnet, & with\ beam\ dump \end{cases} \tag{1} $$
